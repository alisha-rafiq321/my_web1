FAQ
Common Questions

Quick answers to questions we hear most often. Can't find what you need? Reach out directly.

Contact us instead
What is your typical project timeline?
Timelines vary by scope. A standard web application takes 6–10 weeks, while enterprise solutions can range from 3–6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase with clear milestones.
Do you work with clients outside Bahrain?
Yes, we serve clients across the GCC and internationally. Our team is experienced in remote collaboration and follows agile methodologies to ensure seamless delivery regardless of location.
What technologies do you specialize in?
We work with React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, .NET, Java, AWS, Azure, GCP, Docker, Kubernetes, TensorFlow, Power BI, and more. We choose the optimal stack based on your project's specific requirements and long-term goals.
How do you handle data security and compliance?
Security is embedded in our development lifecycle. We follow ISO 27001 standards, conduct regular penetration testing, and ensure compliance with Bahrain PDPL, UAE PDPL, and GDPR. All projects undergo threat modeling and secure code reviews.
What is your pricing model?
We offer fixed-price, time & materials, and retainer models. After understanding your requirements in detail, we recommend the most suitable approach with fully transparent pricing and no hidden costs.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Absolutely. We offer tiered SLA-based support packages — from basic bug fixes to 24/7 dedicated support with incident management. All packages include security patches and performance monitoring.
Can you augment our existing IT team?
Yes, staff augmentation is one of our core services. Our engineers integrate seamlessly with your workflows, tools, and communication channels. We also provide knowledge transfer and upskilling sessions for your in-house team.
