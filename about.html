Our Story

Pioneering Digital Solutions in the GCC

Founded in 2016 in Manama, Kingdom of Bahrain, JSONTECH was born from a simple observation: Gulf enterprises deserved the same caliber of technology partners that Fortune 500 companies take for granted. We set out to bridge that gap — combining global engineering standards with deep regional business understanding.

What started as a five-person DevOps consultancy has grown into a multi-disciplinary technology firm serving 150+ clients across Bahrain, UAE, and Saudi Arabia — from government ministries and banking institutions to logistics giants and retail chains.

Our Delivery Philosophy

  • Consultative Discovery: We immerse ourselves in your operations before writing a single line of code — ensuring every feature maps to a real business outcome.
  • Agile Execution: Two-week sprints with live demos keep stakeholders in the loop and eliminate costly late-stage surprises.
  • Future-Proof Architecture: Microservices, containerized deployments, and API-first design mean your investment scales without rewrites.
